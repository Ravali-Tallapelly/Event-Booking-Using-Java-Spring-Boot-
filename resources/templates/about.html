Frequently Asked Questions

EventBooking is an online platform where users can browse, book, and manage tickets for various tech events and conferences. We aim to make the process of finding and attending tech events easy and hassle-free.

To register for an event, simply create an account on EventBooking, browse the events, select the one you're interested in, and click on the "Register" button. You'll receive an email confirmation and further details about the event.

You can book multiple events by adding them to your cart and proceeding to checkout for each event individually. You will receive a confirmation email for each successful booking.

Yes, you can cancel your registration for any event before the event date. Simply log into your account, navigate to your bookings, and click on the "Cancel" button. Please check our cancellation policy for specific terms.

We accept various payment methods including Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, etc.), PayPal, and Bank Transfers. All payments are processed securely.

You can contact our support team by emailing ravalitallapelly@gmail.com or by visiting the Contact Us page to submit a query directly. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
